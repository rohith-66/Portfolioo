What I built

  • Built a multi-page Power BI dashboard analyzing 15,600+ football injury records across Europe’s top leagues to quantify player availability loss, club-level injury impact, and high-risk player profiles.
  • Performed data cleaning, validation, and aggregation in SQL (MySQL), defining KPIs such as days missed, injury severity, and risk concentration, and cross-validating all dashboard metrics against SQL queries.
  • Designed decision-focused visualizations (KPI summaries, club impact analysis, player risk profiling, age/position severity patterns) to communicate insights to non-technical stakeholders.
